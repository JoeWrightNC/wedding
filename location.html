CEREMONY

The ceremony will take place in the backyard of the property. Please park in the driveway if you intend on staying for most of the evening, or in the cul-de-sac (blocking no driveways) if you intend on leaving earlier so you will not be blocked in. Follow the end of the drive way to the brick path where you'll see a sign directing you to a few pre-ceremony activities and the ceremony seating area (rain or shine)!


CELEBRATION

Immediately following the ceremony, please join us just a few feet away around the pool. We'll be smoking pork, portabella mushrooms, corn on the cob, and heirloom tomatoes and serving it with a low country boil, potato salad, pasta salad, baked beans, fresh fruit, beer and wine! Enjoy some lawn games as we make our way over. As the sun goes down and into the evening we'll have a bonfire, a smores bar, great music, and, of course, wedding cake.

THINGS TO DO AROUND TOWN BEFORE THE CEREMONY

Five out of Six of our suggestions are within 3 blocks from each other in historic downtown Fuquay, located just a couple of miles from the ceremony location.
Aviator Brewery

Fuquay's most popular brewpub, located in the heart of the downtown Varina sidewalk district. A must stop!

Stickboy Bread Company

For a guaranteed delight from some of the most delicious sweet treats you've ever had, stop by Stickboy!

Mason Jar Lager Company

Thats right, a local brewery owned and operated by the iconic Mason Jar company. Stop by for a pretty decent brew!

Fuquay Mineral Spring Park

The very mineral spring that Fuquay was founded around in 1909, now a park with waters believed to have healing powers.

Fainting Goat Brewing Company

Yet another great brewery located in the Fuquay-Varina area. This isa great spot to refresh before the ceremony.

The Mill

Stop by the awesome space at The Mill to enjoy a great cup of craft coffee, a craft brew, or a great cocktail.
